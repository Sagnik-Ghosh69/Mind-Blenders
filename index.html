Aerial Photography

It is the photographs from an aibrcraft or other flying objects like UAV, balloons,kites, parachute,etc.

Aerial Videography

It is a process of capturing or recording a video from a certain height by varying certain projections like vertical videography, low and high oblique videographies,etc.

Underwater Photography

It is the process of taking photographs while underwater. It usually done while scuba diving, but can be done while diving on surface supply, snorkeling swimming, submarsible or remotely operated UWV.

Underwater Videography

It is the process of recording video while underwater. It usually done while scuba diving, but can be done while diving on surface supply, snorkeling swimming, submarsible or remotely operated UWV.
